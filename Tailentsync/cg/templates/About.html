About Us

No.1 Tech based Career Counselling Platform

Who we are


Mere Mentor is an EdTech organization delivering
career counselling services to the students and youth of
the country. We deliver our services to individuals
as well as educational systems and
institutions to explore the factors that would help them to align their
career path in the future, based on their current quests in life.


Our products are shaped for, and cater to the following groups:


  • Grade 8 th - 9 th
  • Grade 10th - 12th
  • College Graduates
  • Working professionals
